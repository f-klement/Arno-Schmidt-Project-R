Mapping the life and work of Arno Schmidt
A GIS project by Florian Klement & David Siegl

Arno Schmidt (1914-1979) was a German author and translator. He is little known outside of German-speaking areas,
in part because his works present a formidable challenge to translators. Although he is not one of the popular authors within modern German
literature of the 20th century, critics and writers often consider him to be one of the most important German-language writers of the 20th century.
